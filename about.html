Who We Are

Since 2007, Halt Group has established itself as a leader in the construction and services sector in Djibouti, offering excellence across various industries with a commitment to quality and innovation.

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Halt Group began with a vision to transform the construction landscape in Djibouti. Through dedication, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence, we have grown from a small operation into one of the region's most trusted construction and services groups.

Today, we operate through specialized subsidiaries, each dedicated to excellence in their respective fields: Halt Construction & Equipment, D-MIX Concrete Plant, and E.A.D Interim Agency.

Leadership & International Partnerships

Our success is built on strong leadership and strategic international partnerships. We collaborate with global industry leaders to bring cutting-edge technology, expertise, and best practices to the Djibouti market.

These partnerships enable us to deliver world-class solutions while maintaining our commitment to local development and employment. Our leadership team combines international experience with deep understanding of the regional construction landscape.

Our Headquarters

Strategically located in the prestigious Heron neighborhood of Djibouti City, our headquarters stand as a symbol of our growth and success in the region.

Completed in 2022, our six-story modern office building is a landmark in one of Djibouti's most sought-after business districts. The Halt Group occupies the entire 6th floor, where our executive offices and strategic planning teams operate.

Each floor of the building is occupied by some of Djibouti's most productive and influential companies, making our headquarters a true hub of business excellence and economic activity in the city. This prime location reflects our position as a leader in Djibouti's construction and development sector.
